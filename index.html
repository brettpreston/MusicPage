Rustic Audio:

Rust-based DSP for voice cleaning

by Brett Preston


It allows you to record voice, apply a DSP effects chain tailored to cleaning voice recordings, and encode it as a highly compressed Opus file. It was created for settings where bandwidth is scarce, such as powerful privacy-preserving communication, but we still want to preserve the legibility and quality of voice recording. Any project pursuing low-footprint, efficiently computed and good quality voice recordings may be interested in using it.
Created with the support of NLnet foundation.

Available at http://github.com/brettpreston/Rustic_Audio,
and https://crates.io/crates/rustic_audio_tool.
